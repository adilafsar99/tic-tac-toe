The program will have four initial modules:

1) Player   
2) Board
3) Game
4) DisplayController

1) Player:
   i) Properties:
      - name
      - mark
      - score
      - isAi

   ii) Methods:
      - getName()
      - setName()
      - getScore()
      - incrementScore()

2) Board:
   i) Properties:
      - board
      - rows
      - colums

   ii) Methods:
      - createBoard()     
      - cloneBoard()
      - checkForWin()
      - isBoardEmpty()

3) Game:
   i) Properties:
      - round
      - currentPlayer
      
   ii) Methods:
      - changeRound()
      - changeCurrentPlayer()
      - playRound()

4) DisplayController:
   i) Methods:
      - updateScreen()
      - handleClick()
